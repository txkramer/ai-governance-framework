## Section 1: AI Model Details

1. What AI models power this product? (e.g., proprietary, GPT-4, open-source models)
2. Where does model inference run? (vendor cloud, third-party cloud, on-premise option)
3. How frequently is the model updated or retrained?
4. Do you use customer data to train or fine-tune your models?
   - If yes, can customers opt out?
   - If opt-out is available, is it applied retroactively?
5. Does the product use third-party AI APIs? If so, which ones, and what data is sent to them?

## Section 2: Data Handling

6. What customer data does the AI feature process? (input data, metadata, usage patterns)
7. How long is customer data retained after processing?
8. Where is customer data stored geographically?
9. Is customer data encrypted in transit and at rest? What encryption standards?
10. Can customer data be fully deleted on request? What is the timeline?
11. Do you maintain data processing agreements? Can you provide a copy?
12. Is there a data flow diagram showing how customer data moves through your AI pipeline?

## Section 3: Security

13. Do you hold SOC 2 Type II certification (or equivalent)?
14. When was your last third-party security audit?
15. How do you handle security vulnerabilities in your AI components?
16. What access controls govern who at your organization can view customer data?
17. Do you support SSO and role-based access for customer administrators?
18. Describe your incident response process, including customer notification timelines.

## Section 4: Compliance

19. Is your product HIPAA-compliant? Will you sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA)?
20. Is your product FERPA-compliant (if applicable to education customers)?
21. How do you handle requests from regulated industries with specific data requirements?
22. Do you maintain documentation mapping your controls to common frameworks (SOC 2, NIST, ISO 27001)?
23. How do you handle government or law enforcement data requests involving customer data?

## Section 5: Transparency & Control

24. Can customers see what data was sent to the AI model for a given output?
25. Do you provide audit logs of AI interactions?
26. Can administrators disable AI features for specific users or departments?
27. How do you handle AI errors or harmful outputs? Is there a feedback mechanism?
28. Do you publish a transparency report or changelog for AI model updates?

## Section 6: Output & Liability

29. Who owns the intellectual property of AI-generated outputs?
30. Does your terms of service include indemnification for AI-generated content?
31. What guardrails exist to prevent the AI from generating harmful, biased, or inaccurate content?
32. How do you test for bias in your AI models?
33. What is your process if a customer reports that AI output caused harm or a compliance violation?

## Section 7: Business Continuity

34. What happens to customer data if the AI feature is discontinued?
35. What is your SLA for the AI-powered features specifically?
36. Do you have a data export option if the customer terminates the contract?
37. What is your disaster recovery plan for the AI infrastructure?
