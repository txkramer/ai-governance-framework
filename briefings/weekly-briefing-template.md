# Weekly AI Briefing Template

Use this format to keep leadership and stakeholders informed about AI initiatives without overwhelming them. Aim for 5-10 minutes of reading time. Send on Monday mornings.

---

## AI Weekly Briefing — [Week of DATE]

**Prepared by:** [Name]
**Distribution:** [Leadership team, AI committee, department heads]

---

*One sentence summarizing the most important AI development this week, either internal or external.*

> Example: "We completed the pilot of [Tool] in the billing department, reducing invoice processing time by 35%."

*1-2 notable AI news items relevant to our industry or operations. Keep it brief and explain why it matters to us.*

---

## Tips for Writing Good Briefings

**Do:**
- Lead with what matters most
- Use plain language, not jargon
- Connect external news to internal impact
- Be honest about setbacks and delays
- Keep it to one page if possible

**Don't:**
- Bury bad news at the bottom
- Include technical details leadership doesn't need
- Skip the briefing because "nothing happened" (say that instead)
- Use it as a status report for every task (focus on decisions and impact)
